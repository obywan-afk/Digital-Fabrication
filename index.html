Digital Fabrication

Week 1

Ideation phase

Old project reusability

Connection with Study

During my master's studies in Paris-Saclay, I learned about the potential of tangible devices and I thought about ways to implement this.

Novelity

I thought about creating a clock that would be 3D printed. The clock should be able to meet all the requirements of the class but could be extended. I considered different design patterns for my room and eventually settled on a circular design. A 3D printed ball with a magnet (or similiar) could be used to create a cirucit which would allow the system to turn on or off an alarm. By placing the ball on different times on the watch, a new alarm could be set. This would make it less static and interactive. The GPIO pins could be used for additional logic (possible shift register required) ( further a light and display with a weather indication could be added) ( maybe even a small mirror)

Preparation

Setting up enviroment. Installing out GitLab. Installing Sharp3D. Installing Prusa Slicer.
